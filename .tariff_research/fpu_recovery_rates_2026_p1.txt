NOTICE TO CUSTOMERS
On October 1, 2025, in Docket 20250121-GU, Florida Public Utilities Company (“FPUC” or the
“Company”) filed a petition with the Florida Public Service Commission (“Commission”)
requesting approval to implement a Natural Gas Facility Relocation Cost Recovery Clause
(“NGFRCRC”) surcharge pursuant to the Rule 25-7.150 of the Florida Administrative Code
enabling companies to recover the costs associated with relocating or reconstructing facilities
that the companies have been required to move by governmental entities.
The Commission approved the surcharge on February 3, 2026. As a result, effective March 1,
2026, the Base Energy Charge applicable to customers served under the rate schedules listed in
the table below will be adjusted to reflect recovery under the NGFRCRC.
The table below reflects the cost recovery factors approved by the Commission including the
applicable tax multiplier, which apply to the ten‑month period from March 1, 2026, through
December 31, 2026.
